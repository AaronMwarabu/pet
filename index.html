My best friend

He is also a big fan of my treat songs, which have such incredibly deep lyrics as: “Would you liiiike a treat-treat-treat, treat-treat-treat? You are good and you are sweet. Would you like a little treat?” C’mon, if you were a dog you’d be excited too.
